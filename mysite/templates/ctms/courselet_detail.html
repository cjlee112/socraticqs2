Threads

Your courselet is made up of threads. A thread starts with a short message, often a brief introduction and a question. Your students will answer the question, read your answer, and assess how similar they are.

If they got it wrong, they’ll see a list of common misconceptions. They’ll compare their answer with the misconceptions as they try to understand how they got it wrong. This is the beauty of active learning – they’re engaged, compare, analyze, and practice critical thinking.
